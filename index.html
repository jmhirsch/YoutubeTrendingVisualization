Visualizing YouTube Trending Videos
This project is a visualization of YouTube trending videos (2021-22).
Explore what factors impact trending videos by filtering trending video data taken from the YouTube API based on video category, country, timeline, and metric (views, likes, dislikes, comments). Then, our visualization tool will display this filtered data for you!
We intend to help content creators better understand what currently drives videos to become trending, and provide them with insights into how they can optimize their own content.
